Car Description

Everyone knows Tesla, so no introduction needed. Their Model 3 has become very popular in recent years.

First: If you stand between the Model Y and the Model 3, these are actually quite similar, but the Model 3 is classified as a lower Sedan type. It also has a luggage cover and not a luggage hatch. Otherwise, it is a bit of a matter of taste which car people like to drive the most, here there are slightly different opinions. But again: Mostly the differences are smaller and if you choose the Model 3, you get a very good all-round car.

Among various functionalities, we can mention 561 liters of luggage space and a potential range of up to 602 kilometers under optimal range conditions. The car's four-wheel drive has two independent motors for improved redundancy. Foreign words, but in short it ensures better road grip and traction control. And safety is of course paramount in the car.
